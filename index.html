Tap dice to build a pool; the number on the left is how many each tap adds. Hold a die for advantage, exploding, and the rest. Any number of sides works, from d1 to d1000, so a d17 or a d57 is just as valid. Rolls stay on your device.

Add this to your home screen and it works offline, with no connection needed.
